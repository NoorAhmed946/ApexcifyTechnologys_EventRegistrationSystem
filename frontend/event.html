The Information Layer

Join us for an immersive journey into the intersection of generative technology and spatial design. "The Information Layer" isn't just a conference; it's a living laboratory hosted at the historic Pier 48. Over the course of three days, we explore how data visualization and architectural lighting are reshaping our urban environments.

Our 2024 summit features over 40 keynote speakers, 12 interactive installation zones, and a dedicated 'Neon Lab' where participants can experiment with real-time reactive lighting arrays. Whether you're a developer, designer, or urban planner, this event provides the raw materials to build the next generation of illuminated experiences.
